About

I’m a software engineer with a background in Computer Science and Physiology/Pharmacology. I like working on projects that are both practical and challenging, whether that means building out a complete web application, experimenting with networking in Java, or analyzing real-world data with Python. Some of the projects I’ve built include an Electronic Medical Record system, a networked multiplayer checkers game, a stock price dashboard, and a soccer match analytics tool.

My main strengths are in React, Node/Express, SQL, and Python, and I’m also comfortable with Docker and setting up CI/CD pipelines. I enjoy taking ideas from concept to working software and focusing on making things reliable, user-friendly, and easy to build on.
